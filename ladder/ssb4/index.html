How it Works

Ranks are based off how high your Rating is. Your Rating is a numerical value representing your skill. The ladder is separated into Tiers that group together players with similar Ratings: S, A, B, and C. The higher your Tier is, the faster your Rating will change after playing matches.

Rewards

  • The Top-5 players on the ladder will be given free entry to any tournament hosted by the Club.
  • The player in 1st place will be given custom art of their main character.

How to Enter

The price for entry is $5. You may pay this amount to a Club Official. Note that only registered Leeward Community College students may enter. New entrants will be given a neutral skill rating. Their rating on the website will be shown after their first three matches.
